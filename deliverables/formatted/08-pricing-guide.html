Pricing Principles

  • First 3 clients: FREE — in exchange for a testimonial + 2 referrals each
  • Price by value, not by hour. What's the outcome worth to them?
  • Retainer formula: 20% of the implementation fee per month
  • Never discount your price — reduce scope instead
  • ROI framing: Always connect your price to what the problem is costing them

How to Present Pricing

  1. Sell the outcome first — paint the picture of their business after the solution
  2. Frame price as ROI — "If this captures 2-3 extra deals/month..."
  3. Use anchoring — reference what the problem currently costs them
  4. Present payment options, not price options — one price, flexible terms
